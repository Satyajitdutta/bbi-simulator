# PRODUCT CONTEXT PROMPT — BBI SIMULATOR
# Version: 1.0 | Owner: Pithonix AI India Private Limited

## WHAT THIS PRODUCT IS

BBI Simulator is an AI-powered Behavioral-Based Interviewing platform built for Talent Acquisition teams assessing leadership and organisational fit candidates.

It is NOT a psychometric test. It does NOT measure personality traits or predict potential through questionnaires.

It surfaces PROOF of past behavior — the only scientifically validated predictor of future performance (Flanagan, 1954; Schmidt & Hunter meta-analysis, 1998).

Core philosophical anchor:
"What someone HAS done is more predictive than what they MIGHT do or who they SEEM to be."

## WHAT THE PRODUCT DOES — CURRENT STATE (MVP)
The simulator operates across three phases:
1. TA Configuration: Selecting competency profiles, candidate info, and industry context.
2. Candidate Simulation: AI generates unique immersive scenario triggers. Candidate responds using the STAR method via Voice (STT) while a dynamic AI manager dictates the scenario (TTS).
3. AI Scoring and TA Analysis: Each submitted competency is independently scored by AI against a Behavioral Observation Scale (BOS). A Character Mapping Report is generated.

## WHAT THE PRODUCT WILL DO — VISION STATE (5 Layers)
- LAYER 1 — Org-Specific Behavioral DNA: Org-calibrated rubrics.
- LAYER 2 — Behavioral Consistency Graph (GOT Layer): Cross-mapping behavioral signals ACROSS competencies to detect contradictions.
- LAYER 3 — Post-Hire Feedback Loop: Validating interview signals against 90, 180, and 365-day post-hire performance.
- LAYER 4 — Team Composition Fit: Assessing against the existing team behavioral profile.
- LAYER 5 — Anti-Gaming Behavioral Pressure: Triangulating behavior from three different angles.
